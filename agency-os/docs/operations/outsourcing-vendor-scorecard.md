# Outsourcing Vendor Scorecard

## Objective
- Standardize vendor evaluation to reduce rework and missed deadlines.

## Dimensions and Weights
- On-time delivery: 25%
- Quality/rework rate: 30%
- Communication responsiveness: 20%
- Risk awareness and escalation: 15%
- Documentation completeness: 10%

## Scoring Scale
- 5: Excellent
- 4: Good
- 3: Acceptable
- 2: Needs improvement
- 1: Unacceptable

## Score Interpretation
- >= 4.2: Preferred vendor
- 3.5 - 4.1: Approved vendor
- 2.8 - 3.4: Conditional vendor (improvement plan required)
- < 2.8: Do not assign critical work

## Governance Rules
- Evaluate every completed milestone
- Require remediation plan for any score < 3.0
- Rotate away from vendors with two consecutive low scores
- Execution handoff and collaboration details are in `docs/operations/outsourcing-playbook.md`.
