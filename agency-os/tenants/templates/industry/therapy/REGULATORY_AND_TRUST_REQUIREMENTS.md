# Industry Overlay - Therapy / Wellness (Regulatory and Trust Requirements)

> Apply this overlay for art therapy / counseling / wellness service sites.

## Trust and Safety Baseline

- Required pages:
  - Privacy Policy
  - Service Scope and Disclaimer (not emergency service)
  - Contact and escalation resources
  - Consent/intake notice (if intake forms are used)
- Data minimization:
  - Collect only necessary personal data
  - Do not collect sensitive health details unless required
  - Define retention/deletion rules
